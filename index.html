With a steady hand and expert know-how, plasterers can complete the tricky art of plastering walls in new properties as well as commercial building renovations. For every surface that needs to be covered, there are two coats needed: first coat is for making wallboard while second one will cover it all up with cement for an extra layer of protection.The plasterer is typically responsible for applying plaster on internal surfaces, ceilings and any other textures which are not necessarily structural. This way, they can achieve a decorative effect with no additional labor cost.
